About

Our story

Everyone has a story to tell and like all humble beginnings, FoodHunts Restaurant started open in January 2018. For the online delivery food, we started in May 2018.

As a university student we faced problem of buying food in the university campus on some specific occassion, to solve those problem we come out with FoodHunts. We want to provide tasty food to the customer within a quick time and with a easy payment system

Our business is not only about providing quick meals. It’s always been about you – your tastes, your lifestyle, your choices. And we’d like to keep it that way, with you lovin’ every moment of it.

This is why we put our heart and soul into each and every one of our delicious, wholesome meals. Each ‘mmm’ and ‘ahhh’ from you is thanks enough. As you change and grow, we’ll be with you every step of the way, as the FoodHunts you have come to know and love, Today and tomorrow.
